The Agricultural Economics Archive

A curated hub for essential datasets and economic intelligence.

Global Production & Statistics

  • FAOSTAT (UN FAO) The gold standard for global agriculture data: production, trade, land use, and food security indicators.
  • World Bank: Agriculture & Rural Development Macro-economic indicators including agricultural value added (% of GDP) and rural employment stats.

Markets, Prices & Projections

  • USDA Economic Research Service (ERS) Essential for U.S. and global commodity outlooks, farm income forecasts, and trade data.
  • AMIS (Agricultural Market Information System) Inter-agency platform focusing on market transparency for wheat, maize, rice, and soybeans.
  • GIEWS Food Price Monitoring Tool for monitoring domestic food prices in over 80 countries.

Policy & Research Portals

  • IFPRI Open Data International Food Policy Research Institute's repository for household surveys and food policy modeling.
  • Unified Portal for Agricultural Statistics (UPAg) Real-time information on crop estimates, prices, and procurement (India-specific but a model for data portals).
